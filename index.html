The story behind the pursuit of athletic excellence.

I have the priviledge of actively supporting my son while he trains and competes in swimming and watching him grow in the sport over the last eight years. During this time I have tested and evolved many different approaches and ideas for bringing out the best in my star athlete. The idea behind creating this website was to be able to share some of my learnings as I supported my child first as he was learning to swim all the way through to now competing as a top performing athlete. Everything you will read has been hands on learning together with lots of reading.

Athletic performance is not just getting off the couch and going for a quick run or swim. By contrast peak athletic performance is composed of the six key elements, each just as important as each other and each playing a very specific role.

They are:

  1. Balanced nutriton appropriate for the specific sport.
  2. Cardiovasular conditioning.
  3. Mastering the skills and techniques unique to each sport.
  4. Building strength to advance explosive performance.
  5. Training for speed.
  6. Flexibility to increase performance and avoid injury.
